## Technology & Magic System

### Harmonic Resonance Basics

#### Core Concepts
- **All is frequency:** Reality = vibrations (441.3 Hz baseline for Jason)
- **Matter = stable eigenresonance:** Coherent frequency patterns
- **Harmonic field:** Shaped by planetary/solar/galactic motions
- **Inflection:** Resonant pattern introduced to mana field

#### Three Key Variables (P2-01)
1. **Invest:** Directed strain/effort (comes from user)
2. **Quality:** Harmonic alignment with environment
3. **Firmness:** Structural tolerance (how much pressure it holds)

#### 9 Resonance Types (circular XY-plane)
- Vital (0�): Life, growth
- Aether (40�): Spirit, transcendent
- Flame (80�): Heat, combustion (Lina's strength)
- Stone (120�): Weight, durability
- Flow (160�): Water, movement
- Storm (200�): Air, lightning
- Light (240�): Clarity, purity
- Shadow (280�): Obscuration, entropy
- Mind (320�): Will, perception

**Note:** These are abstractions, not true categories - everything is a continuum

### Safety Systems

#### Green/Yellow/Orange/Red (established P2-01)
- **Green:** Fine, continue
- **Yellow:** Uncomfortable, caution
- **Orange:** Getting bad, prepare to withdraw (10min limit)
- **Red:** STOP IMMEDIATELY, no exceptions

#### Symptoms by Level
- **Green:** No symptoms
- **Yellow:** Pressure behind eyes, mild headache
- **Orange:** Brow tension, headache, slight nausea
- **Red:** Nosebleed, cognitive impairment, risk of structural damage

#### Recovery Times (P2-04)
- After Yellow: 15 minutes
- After Orange: 1 hour minimum
- After Red: Several hours to 1 day
- After Scaffold (extreme): 3-4 days mandatory

### Whisper Clips (established P2-07)

#### Physical Design
- Clear plastic ear-hook
- Clips behind ear (concha/antihelix region)
- Contact point: Mastoid bone
- Core: Titanium spring + ceramic/quartz disc
- Appearance: Nearly invisible, looks like hearing aid

#### Capabilities
- Bone conduction audio from RAE
- Range: ~10m sphere around Jason (current max)
- Max channels: 3 simultaneous (stable), 2 easy, 1 effortless
- No electronics, no logs, passive only
- Tuned to each wearer's specific frequency

#### Protocols
- Floor control: Half-duplex (one speaker at a time)
- Handshake phrase on join: "low tide"
- Tap gesture for floor request
- RAE can interrupt anyone for warnings
- Auto-close if Jason hits Orange

#### Cost
- Light use (1-2 channels): 5-10 min fine
- Medium (2 channels, 5-10m): 2-4 min
- Heavy (3 channels AND 8-10m): <60s, then cooldown
- Cooldown: 1-3 min quiet/carrier switch

### Carriers

#### Material Effectiveness (best to worst)
1. **High-Q metals/ceramics:** Great but easy to jam
2. **Wood:** Moderate, good for stealth
3. **Fabric/foam:** Damps nicely, bad for range
4. **Liquids:** Unpredictable, avoid

#### Named Carriers Used
- Kettle (P2-01, first voice)
- Water bottle (P2-01, Lina test)
- Ceramic bowls (P2-06, frequency training)
- Toaster (ongoing mystery)
- Phone (backup, "figleaf" - deprecated)

### Blocking & Interference

#### Hard Lockouts
- Sustained pure single tones (whistle, tuning fork, sine wave)
- Pure melody (street musician motif)
- Jason overload (Red state)
- Carrier swamped by external drive

#### Soft Interference
- Broadband noise (subway, HVAC)
- Wind/rain on clips (hand shielding helps)
- Loose fit / soft damping
- Motion while free-fielding
- Large reverberant volumes

#### Workarounds
1. Switch carrier (room ? clip ? molar)
2. Touch grounded material (wood/metal)
3. Micro-bursts between beats
4. Hand shield over clip
5. Relocate 2 steps
