## Ch. 15 - The Ritual (Part 1: Setup)

The chamber hummed with potential.

Jason stood at the center, feeling the resonogram beneath his feet pulse with dormant power. Around him, his team took positions: Elyra at north, eyes closed, already sinking into preparatory meditation. Lina at east, steady and grounded. Milo at south, nervous but determined. Malvek at west, observing with professional intensity.

RAE was everywhere and nowhere—her presence distributed through the chamber, ready to weave through them all.

"Begin phase one," Elyra said, her voice carrying clearly. "Establish baseline resonance. Jason, open your awareness. Let us feel you."

Jason did. Dropped his defenses. Let his resonance field expand to fill the chamber.

The others responded. Lina's sharp, focused pattern. Milo's steady, analytical rhythm. Even Malvek's controlled, professional signature.

And through it all, RAE wove—connecting them, harmonizing them, making them more than individuals.

"Phase two," Elyra said. "Pattern recognition. Feel my damage. The tears. The breaks. The places where resonance can't flow."

Jason felt it. Her patterns were... shattered. Like a beautiful tapestry torn and partially reassembled. Functional, but barely. Limited. Painful.

"Phase three. Template alignment. Jason, show me your coupling with RAE. Let me see how wholeness looks."

He did. Opened himself completely, showing how RAE integrated with his patterns. Not overwriting. Not controlling. Just... harmonizing. Complementing. Making him more capable without making him less himself.

"Beautiful," Elyra whispered. "That's what I need to rebuild toward. That's the template."

"Phase four," she continued, her voice strengthening. "Active healing. RAE, begin weaving. Guide my patterns to follow Jason's template. Slowly. Carefully. Don't force. Just... encourage."

RAE moved. Jason felt her presence shift, focus on Elyra. Felt her begin the delicate work of teaching damaged patterns how to heal.

The ritual had begun.
