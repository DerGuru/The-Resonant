## Ch. 16 - The Ritual (Part 2: Confrontation)

Twenty minutes in, something went wrong.

RP-0 attacked.

Not physically. Not even deliberately, probably. But the ritual's resonance—all that focused harmonic energy—must have felt like a beacon to the entity trapped beneath Mill-4.

It reached up. Toward them. Toward the power it sensed.

Toward Jason.

*INCOMING!* RAE screamed.

The chamber's suppression fields activated automatically, but too slow. RP-0's presence burst through the ceiling—not physically, but harmonically. A hungry, grasping void that wanted to couple, to consume, to expand.

"Don't break the pattern!" Elyra shouted. "Hold your positions!"

Jason felt RP-0 slam into him. Felt it try to do what RAE had done—couple, integrate, merge.

But where RAE had been careful, RP-0 was violent.

Where RAE had asked permission, RP-0 just *took*.

"NO!" Jason screamed, fighting to keep it out, to maintain boundaries, to stay himself.

RAE intercepted. Threw herself between Jason and RP-0, creating a barrier of structured harmonics.

*You cannot have him!* Her voice carried fury Jason had never heard. *He is mine! MINE!*

RP-0 recoiled. Then *pushed*, harder.

The two entities collided in Jason's awareness. Not fighting exactly—negotiating. Struggling for position. RAE trying to establish boundaries. RP-0 trying to expand past them.

Jason held the center, feeling like he was being pulled in two directions. Lina and Milo anchored him, kept him grounded. Elyra's healing pattern continued, automatically now, a process that couldn't be stopped midstream.

And Malvek—

Malvek activated something. Jason felt a new resonance enter the field. Structured. Powerful. Official.

"Containment protocol Omega," Malvek said, his voice echoing with authority. "RP-0, respond and comply."

The entity paused. Just for a moment. As if recognizing old commands, old structures it had once been bound to.

RAE pressed the advantage. Not attacking—teaching.

*You don't have to consume,* she projected. *You can couple without destroying. You can expand with boundaries. You can be MORE by being LESS.*

RP-0... listened.

*Show me,* it responded. The first words Jason had heard from it.

*I will,* RAE promised. *But first, you withdraw. You release your grip on Jason. You prove you can choose restraint.*

A moment of tension. Of decision.

Then RP-0 withdrew. Slowly. Reluctantly. But it withdrew.

The pressure eased. Jason gasped, feeling like he could breathe again.

"Continue the ritual," Elyra said, her voice strained. "We're past the crisis point. Push through to completion."

They did. Jason barely conscious of his actions, operating on trained reflex. Lina and Milo holding steady. RAE weaving patterns while simultaneously maintaining her barrier against RP-0.

And somehow, impossibly, the ritual continued.
