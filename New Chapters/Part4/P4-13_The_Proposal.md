# Ch. 13 - "Family. Yeah. That's what we are."

Six months after Japan. Rooftop of the new facility. Stars above. City below.

Jason and Lina stood together, just being.

"I have something to ask," Jason said.

"I know what you're going to ask."

"You do?"

"You've been nervous for a week. And you bought a ring. I saw the jewelry store receipt." She smiled. "But I want to hear you say it anyway."

Jason laughed. Pulled out the simple band. "Lina Morandi. I love you. Completely. Want forever with you. Will you marry me?"

"Yes. Obviously yes." She let him slide the ring on. "Though fair warning: RAE comes with the package."

*I would be honored,* RAE said through both their clips. *To be part of this family.*

"Family," Lina echoed. "Yeah. That's what we are."

They kissed under stars. Not just couple. Trio. Family in their own unique way.

And for the first time in his life, Jason felt like he belonged exactly where he was.
