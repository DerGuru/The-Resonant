## Ch. 05 - International Contact

The visitor arrived unannounced but with Malvek's approval.

Dr. Yuki Tanaka. Japanese Resonance Institute. Mid-forties, professional, carrying credentials that were impressive and slightly intimidating.

"Thank you for seeing me," she said, bowing slightly. "I've traveled far to observe your methods."

"Observe?" Jason asked warily.

"Your reformation of RP-0. Your training program. Your partnership with regulatory authority." She smiled. "You've accomplished what many thought impossible. My country faces similar challenges. I hope to learn."

They spent three days showing her their program. Kira's training methods. Elyra's healing progress. The safety protocols they'd developed.

Yuki observed everything with intense focus. Asked thoughtful questions. Demonstrated surprising knowledge of harmonic theory.

On the third day, she revealed her true purpose.

"Japan has dormant entities. Like RP-0, but different. We've contained them, but containment is not solution." She pulled up holographic data. "One is awakening. Designation: Kage-K?z?. Shadow Structure. Unlike RP-0, this one was built deliberately malicious. Warfare research from fifty years ago."

"You want our help reforming it," Lina said.

"No." Yuki's expression darkened. "I want your help destroying it. Some things cannot be reformed. This is one of them."
