# Ch. 07 - "Small, careful changes"

Saturday morning arrived with rain and the smell of wet pavement drifting through Jason's open window.

The city was blurred and shimmering, colors running together in the soft focus of his early morning eyes. He loved this time of day, when the world felt like it was holding its breath, waiting for something to happen.

Today was different, though. Today had a weight to it, a sense of anticipation that set his teeth on edge. He couldn't shake the feeling that he was on the brink of something𤪔e just didn't know what.

Lina had said to be careful. Her words echoed in his mind, a slight frown creasing his forehead.

He pushed himself up from the warmth of his bed, swinging his legs over the side and resting his elbows on his knees. The cool air hit his face, dragging him closer to wakefulness.

Rain pattered softly against the window, a soothing rhythm that made it hard to think about anything but sleep. But he couldn't sleep. Not now.

Not when there were changes brewing, both outside his window and deep within himself.

He thought about Lina, about their conversations that flitted from trivial to profound in the space of a heartbeat. He thought about her laughter, how it seemed to chase the shadows away, if only for a little while.
