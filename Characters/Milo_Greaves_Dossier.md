# Character Profile: Milo Greaves

## Synopsis
Milo is one of Jason’s companions. He’s socially awkward, intelligent, and a self-taught hacker. He’s not elite - tier but skilled enough to break into a city archive, which is how Jason eventually finds some key documents. Milo initially seems suspicious to others but proves to be loyal and resourceful. He tends to dwell in abandoned places and avoids large groups. Despite his introversion, he becomes a reliable support for Jason during their harmonic resonance discovery.

---
## Basic Info
- **Name:** Milo Greaves  
- **Age:** 31  
- **Background:** Grew up in a quiet suburb; self-taught scholar and conspiracy theorist with a flair for forgotten history  
- **Occupation:** Freelance archivist, digital hobbyist, unofficial blogger on "lost magic" lore   
- **Residence:** Tiny cluttered apartment filled with secondhand books, blinking monitors, and coffee mugs

---

## Personality Snapshot
- Cynical but funny; prone to dry one-liners and obscure pop culture references  
- Fiercely curious, with a tendency to go down intellectual rabbit holes for days  
- Avoids confrontation, but will stand his ground with words when pushed  
- Loyal once trust is earned, though often masks sincerity behind sarcasm  

---

## Skills & Specialties
- Solid research skills; can locate lost documents, arcane texts, or long-buried local history  
- Mid-level computer hacking: enough to breach outdated security systems, access municipal or institutional files (e.g., Jason’s city archive), spoof credentials, and dig into restricted databases  
- Excellent at pattern recognition and symbolic interpretation, especially sigils, resonograms or historical spellforms  
- Maintains a vast private digital database on "magical" anomalies and rumors

---

## Connection to Jason
- First contact with Jason begins with conflict: Jason stumbles across a set of mysteriously uploaded documents on a fringe magic forum — documents from his own archive system.
- Second Contact is catching Milo in the archive at "releasing old archived files to the public domain" (he was hacking the archive)
- Milo is unapologetic but intrigued; he didn’t know who Jason was but is now convinced Jason’s involvement isn’t random.
- Despite the rocky start, Milo gradually earns Jason’s trust through help freely given, insight and surprising depth.
- Sees Jason as someone who could validate decades of Milo’s obscure obsessions  
- Over time becomes a close companion and occasional moral compass

---

## Role in the Story
- Provides vital research leads and unlocks technical obstacles (digital or harmonic theory)  
- Balances the team emotionally—snarky, but grounded in realism  
- Occasionally in over his head, but saves the group more often than expected  
- Serves as the bridge between the mundane world and harmonic theory

---

## Arc Potential
- Learns to accept his support role without needing direct talent for inflecting  
- May unlock one minor interaction late in the story (Resonogram creation)  
- Grows from outsider into trusted insider and emotional anchor
