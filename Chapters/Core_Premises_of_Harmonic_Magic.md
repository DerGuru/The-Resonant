
# Core Premises of Harmonic Resonance Magic

---

1. **All is frequency.**  
   Reality is made of vibrations; mana is the resonant field through which they manifest.

2. **Matter is stable eigenresonance.**  
   Matter consists of coherent frequency patterns that sustain themselves through internal harmony.

3. **The mana field is shaped cosmically.**  
   Planetary, solar, and galactic motions create a layered, ever-shifting harmonic resonance.

4. **Magic is resonance.**  
   Spells are temporary, resonant patterns introduced into the mana field to cause effects.

5. **Quality is harmony.**  
   A spell's durability and efficiency depend on:
   - **Harmonic Alignment** (fit with the environment)
   - **Eigenresonance** (internal cohesion)

6. **All things decay through disharmony.**  
   As the ambient resonance drifts over time, even stable patterns face pressure and may unravel.

> Magic is not imposed upon the world. It is played, like music, in tune with the cosmos.
