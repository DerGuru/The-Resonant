### **Ch. 18 - New Equilibrium (Epilogue)**
**Purpose:** Establish final status quo, set up potential future  
**Setting:** One year later, various scenes  
**Conflict:** None—resolution  

**Key Beats:**

**Training Facility:**
- Twenty students now
- Kira teaching beginners
- Elyra at 25% capacity, senior instructor
- Jason/Lina co-directors
- Milo coordinates with Malvek's organization

**RP-0:**
- Still contained, but helping remotely
- Prevents cascade failures
- Ethical, contained, useful
- RAE visits (harmonically) weekly

**Japan:**
- Yuki established sister program
- Regular exchanges
- International resonance community forming

**Personal:**
- Jason/Lina married (small ceremony)
- Integration at 80%, stable
- Considering: Could they have children?
- RAE: "I would protect them. Always."

**Malvek:**
- Quarterly meetings, genuine partnership
- Respect earned on both sides
- He asks: "What's next for you?"
- Jason: "We teach. We build. We make this world safer."

**Final Scene:**
- Jason training new student
- Shows them the mug he fixed years ago
- "This is where it started. One small change. One careful choice."
- Student: "Can I really learn this?"
- Jason: "If you're patient. If you're ethical. If you choose precision over power."
- Camera pulls back: Facility humming with life
- Multiple teachers, multiple students
- Future being built, one person at a time

**Closing Line:**
*"Progress isn't loud. It's not dramatic. It's just... people helping people. One careful choice at a time. And that, more than any power, is what changes the world."*
