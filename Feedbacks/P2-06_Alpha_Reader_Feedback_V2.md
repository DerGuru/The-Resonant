## What Works Brilliantly

### ? Title: "We are" (Masterful)

**Why This Title Is Perfect:**

**Philosophical Depth:**
- Not "We were" (past) or "We will be" (future)
- "We **are**" (present, absolute, existential)
- Descartes: "I think therefore I am"
- Here: "We are" (existence without individual "I")
- Ontological shift in two words

**From the Text:**
```
*This is,* he thought. Or was it RAE thinking it?

**We are,** came the response. But it wasn't in RAE's voice. It was in his voice. Their voice.
```

**Perfect Because:**
- Title is literally spoken during the experience
- Not narrator description but CHARACTER voice
- The moment identity becomes plural
- Grammatically simple, philosophically profound

**Contrast to Normal Communication:**
- Usually: *Jason thinks* ? **RAE responds** (two voices)
- Here: *This is* ? **We are** (one voice)
- No dialogue, only unified consciousness
- Shows transformation in grammar itself

**Series Context:**
- Ch. 04: "She knows something **we** don't" (plural but separate)
- Ch. 05: "Nineteen points from permanent" (measurement)
- Ch. 06: **"We are"** (merged entity)
