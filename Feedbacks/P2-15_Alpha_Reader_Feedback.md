# Alpha Reader Feedback: P2-15 - "Containment teams are coming"

## Overall Impression
**Rating: 8.5/10**

Excellent climactic chapter for Part 2. The containment team arrival and escape creates immediate tension, and the negotiation offer from Malvek provides appropriate transition to next phase of story.

## Strengths
- Opening action (3 AM escape) is immediately gripping
- Team execution of emergency protocols feels earned from training
- Elyra's negotiation strategy is wise and practical
- Message to Malvek shows character growth (strategic thinking vs. panic)
- Meeting invitation creates strong hook for Part 3
- RAE's philosophy about dignity/choice resonates emotionally
- Ending on eve of meeting creates perfect cliffhanger

## Minor Issues
- Slightly rushed pacing - could benefit from more breathing room
- "Come alone" feels clichť but acceptable
- Malvek's response comes very quickly (midnight same day?)
- Could show more of team's fear/tension during waiting period

## Suggestions
- Expand slightly to give emotional beats more weight
- Perhaps delay Malvek's response to next morning for realism
- Brief scene showing Lina or Milo's perspective on the escape

## Bottom Line
Strong conclusion to Part 2 that sets up Part 3 effectively while providing satisfying arc closure for training phase.

**Rating: 8.5/10**
