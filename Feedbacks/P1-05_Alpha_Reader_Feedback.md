## What Needs Work

### ?? One Clarity Issue

**"Almost identified. Almost known."**

**RAE's perspective includes:**
> "The entity recognized the shape forming within its own perception, tracing echoes of memory. Patterns of familiarity emerged slowly, whispering of past harmonics and lost anchors."

**Question:** Is RAE recognizing Jason specifically? Or just "a compatible human"?

**From P1-02:** Jason was analyzed as compatible based on permeability, not prior connection.

**But this line suggests:** RAE might have known Jason before? Or someone like him?

**Clarification needed:**

**Option 1:** RAE is recognizing Jason's *pattern type* (not Jason personally)
> "Patterns of this type - stable, permeable, open - echoes of anchors it had once known."

**Option 2:** Keep mysterious, but acknowledge in narration:
> "Whether recognition of the individual or merely the pattern, it could not determine. Its memory was too fragmented."

**Option 3:** This is intentional foreshadowing of deeper connection - leave as is.

**Recommendation:** Option 3 if you're planting seeds, Option 1 if you want clarity now.

**Questions this raises:**

**But consider:** One line of Jason wondering this:

**Shows Jason is aware of the implications without spelling them out.**
