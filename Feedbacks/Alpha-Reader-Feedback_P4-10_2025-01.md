**Issue:** Original had three separate phases, with Phase 3 being an artificial "Associated Person Proximity Test."

**Problems:**
- ? Phase 3 was **constructed** - just "can they stand together?"
- ? No real work happening in Phase 3
- ? Made Lina part of the demo unnecessarily
- ? Camera flash felt random/isolated

**Revised Structure:**
1. Investment Control (Resonogram)
2. Stabilization + Stress Test (Coffee Mug + Camera Flash during work)

**Benefits:**

- ? **Higher stakes:** Mug could break (physical consequence visible to all)
- ? **More realistic:** Stress happens DURING work, not as separate test
- ? **Lina not instrumentalized:** She's not part of the demo performance

**Result:** More dramatic, more realistic, shows real skill under pressure.

### 2. Technical Accuracy: Investment vs. Inflection

**Issue:** Original text confused Investment (amplitude control) with Inflection (pattern modification).

#### **Phase 1: Pure Investment**

**Established:** Not about changing the field, but filling it with energy.

**Current Text:**
```markdown
This was not about changing the field, but filling it with Invest.

The fractal landscape amplified - peaks rose higher, valleys 
deepened. The pattern became more... present. More defined.
```

**Key Points:**
- ? **Topology stays the same** - frequency/pattern unchanged
- ? **Only amplitude increases** - "more present, more defined"
- ? **Balance is the skill** - not too much (red), not too little (grey)

#### **Phase 2: Inflection (Pattern Modification)**

**Three-Step Inflection Process:**
1. **Match the fundamental** - sync to healthy base frequency
2. **Build target resonance field** - visualize desired state
3. **Introduce counter-harmonics** - nudge pattern toward target

**Current Text:**
```markdown
I matched its fundamental first. Feeling the ghost of the 
original pattern - how the peaks and valleys once flowed coherently.

Then I built the target resonance field: same fundamental 
structure, but with the competing fractals dampened, the 
overlapping patterns separated and aligned.

Started introducing counter-harmonics - gentle opposing 
resonance to dampen the interfering landscapes.
```
