## Alpha Reader Notes & Insights

### Most Effective Elements:
1. **Probability calculation opening** - Immediately shows 93.7% integration without exposition
2. **RP-0's philosophical precision** - "That is not consent. That is coercion disguised as choice"
3. **Lina's ambivalence** - Makes her real and complex, not just supportive

### Areas That Needed Work:
1. **Timeline math** - Had to recalculate integration progression
2. **Integration commentary** - Too much self-awareness for 93.7%
3. **Ritual facts** - Inconsistencies about planning/approval
4. **Dialogue efficiency** - Repetitive ethics explanations
5. **Lina's passivity** - Needed her own voice and perspective

### Lessons for Future Chapters:

**Integration Portrayal:**
- Show through behavior, not commentary
- Higher integration = less self-reflection about integration
- Accept as baseline, not remarkable phenomenon

**Character Voice:**
- Every character needs agency and complexity
- "Supportive" doesn't mean "passive"
- Ambivalence makes characters real

**Thematic Structure:**
- Make parallels explicit but subtle (bridge passages)
- Let theme emerge from situation, not exposition
- Trust reader to connect philosophical/personal layers

**Dialogue Economy:**
- Say it once, powerfully
- Repetition weakens impact
- Leave space for reader inference
