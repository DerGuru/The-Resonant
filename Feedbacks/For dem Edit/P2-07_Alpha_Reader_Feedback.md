# Alpha Reader Feedback: P2-07 - "Green to yellow, never to red"

## Overall Impression
**Rating: 7.5/10**

This chapter provides satisfying training progression and team development with good pacing and clear skill advancement. The structure is solid, showing incremental growth while maintaining the established safety boundaries. However, it suffers from timeline placement issues and notably fails to address the integration crisis that should be reaching its conclusion around this time.

## Critical Issue: Integration Crisis Timeline

### The Missing Deadline
**P2-05**: Jason had 3-4 weeks before irreversible 60% integration  
**P2-06**: 5 days later, Jason at 40.8%, calculated 33 days to hit 60% at current rate  
**P2-07**: Opens with "Four weeks into training"

**Math problem**:
- If this is 4 weeks from P2-04 (first Mill-4 session), we're approaching or past the integration deadline
- If this is 4 weeks from some other point, when?
- Either way, Jason should be nearing 60% integration at the rate established in P2-06 (0.6% per training day)

**Required**: Either:
1. Show Jason's integration level has stabilized successfully
2. Show he's approaching the deadline and making final decisions
3. Clarify the timeline so we understand where we are relative to the crisis

This is a critical plot thread that can't just disappear.

### Integration Implications for This Chapter
If we're 4 weeks into training with heavy active work (50-meter perception pushing to Red, sustained shaping, deflection drills), Jason's integration should be climbing. Where is he now? Has his disciplined approach worked? Is he still at ~42% or has he crept toward 50%?

**This needs acknowledgment**, even if just a brief mention.
