### 2. **Hospital Records Logic**
**Rating: 4/5 - Works but could be clearer**

Current:
```markdown
"Hospital intake records gave them your name - *Mr. Chen* - and a fake address," Milo said.
```

**Question:** Why would the hospital have a *fake* address? 

**Two interpretations:**
1. Jason gave fake address when admitted (but he was unconscious!)
2. Milo gave fake address (but said "standard procedure")

**Suggested clarification:**
```markdown
"Hospital intake records gave them your name," Milo said. "When I filled out the emergency contact form, I gave them my address, not yours. But they still got your phone number - I had to provide that in case they needed to reach you after discharge."
```

OR if the fake address is intentional:
```markdown
"Hospital intake records gave them your name," Milo said. "I put down a different address when I filled out the paperwork - thought it might help - but I had to give them your real phone number for discharge coordination."
```
